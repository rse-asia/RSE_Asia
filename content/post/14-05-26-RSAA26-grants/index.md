Call for Accessibility Fellows, Scholarships, and Micro-grants for Research Software Asia Australia, Research Software Africa, and Research Software Latin America at Equersa 2026.

<!--more-->

Equersa 2026 is offering Accessibility Fellowships, Scholarships, and Micro-grants for the following group of online research conferences running 25th-28th August 2026:

- [Research Software Asia-Australia (RSAA26)](https://rseaa.org)
- [Research Software Africa (RSAfrica26)](https://www.research-software-africa.org)
- [Research Software Latin America (RSLA26)](https://rs-latam.org)

_Accessibility Fellowships_ aim to encourage individuals with disabilities from within our community or adjacent communities to join one of the three regional conferences.

  <a class="rse rse-join" href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdnHNdGZSpdu1nXkX8AEtGMuzn5saSLTW8vP5Eupwu7TE-wUQ/viewform" target ="_blank"> Apply for accessibility fellowship!</a>

_Scholarships_ provide free conference tickets.

_Micro-grants_ of $50 AUD (USD35 approx.) are intended to help with internet, headphones, childcare etc.

<a class="rse rse-join" href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSciNUkvFkr96CcjrLRv84asri4VGTKTvzi3lqMU0DPFhYsZtA/viewform" target ="_blank"> Apply for scholarship or microgrant!</a>

Applications due:

- Accessibility Fellowships: 26th June 2026
- Scholarships: 14th August 2026
- Micro-grants: 10th July 2026

Please apply and help share the news!

Further details and link to apply: https://equersa.org/#call-for-accessibility-fellows-scholarships-and-micro-grants-for-rsaa-rsafrica-and-rsla
