RSE Asia is proud to announce the signing of its first Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Asia Pacific Advanced Network (APAN). This partnership marks a significant milestone in our mission to promote and support Research Software Engineering (RSE) across the Asia-Pacific region.
