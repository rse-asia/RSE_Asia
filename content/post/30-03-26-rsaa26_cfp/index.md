The Call for Presentations (CFP) for the conferences Research Software Asia Australia 2026 (RSAA26), Research Software Africa 2026 (RSAfrica26), and Research Software Latin America 2026 (RSLA26) at Equersa 2026 are now open. This year’s theme, “Research Software Without Borders,” focuses on fostering collaboration and engagement within the research software community.

- [Research Software Africa (RSAfrica26)](https://www.research-software-africa.org)
