Equersa 2026 - Call for Presentations

Jyoti Bhogal
Mar 30, 2026 1 min read

The Call for Presentations (CFP) for the conferences Research Software Asia Australia 2026 (RSAA26), Research Software Africa 2026 (RSAfrica26), and Research Software Latin America 2026 (RSLA26) at Equersa 2026 are now open. This year’s theme, “Research Software Without Borders,” focuses on fostering collaboration and engagement within the research software community.

  • Research Software Asia-Australia (RSAA26)
  • Research Software Africa (RSAfrica26)
  • Research Software Latin America (RSLA26)

Submit your abstract for CfP!

Applications due:

  • RSAA26: 29th May 2026
  • RSAfrica26: 29th May 2026
  • RSLA26: 31st May 2026

Please apply and help share the news!

Further details and link to apply: Equersa 2026
